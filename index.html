Paraguay

Paraguay is a landlocked country in South America, located between Brazil, Argentina, and Bolivia. It is known for its bilingual culture (Spanish and Guaraní), strong agricultural economy, and its capital Asunción. Nearby, the famous Iguazu Falls, one of the largest and most spectacular waterfall systems in the world, attracts visitors to the region. 🌎💧
